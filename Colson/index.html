Artist Bio

I am Colson Agin, a classical pianist devoted to the works of Bach and Beethoven. My approach blends respect for historical performance practice with a personal, intimate reading of each piece.

From the intricate counterpoint of the Well-Tempered Clavier to the dramatic architecture of Beethoven's sonatas, I seek to communicate the structure, emotion, and clarity of this repertoire through the piano.

This site is a place to share my performances, recordings, and sheet music—a small window into my ongoing work with these composers.
